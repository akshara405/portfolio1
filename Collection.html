SERENE SPACES

Jharoka



This A3 painting blends Udaipur's serene lakes with Jodhpur's majestic fort jharoka, capturing Rajasthan’s natural beauty and royal heritage.

Rasa Leela

"Rasa Leela" depicts the divine dance of Radha and Krishna, surrounded by nature and bathed in sunlight, symbolizing love's radiant power that connects the spiritual and natural worlds in perfect harmony.

A heartfelt painting I created as a gift for my mother, a devoted follower of Radha and Krishna. The divine couple, surrounded by nature and illuminated by sunlight, represent the eternal love and devotion she holds in her heart. Through this artwork, I aimed to express my love for her and celebrate the spiritual bond she shares with the divine.

Raunak



"Raunak" is a beautiful A3-sized painting that captures the essence of life’s radiance. The artwork evokes a sense of tranquility and renewal, reflecting the glowing essence of life in its purest form.

Dahhad



"Dahhad" is a masterful square painting that exquisitely portrays the regal stature of a tigress, embodying the perfect balance between strength and elegance. The tigress, crowned with a majestic tiara, stands as a symbol of royal power and authority. A delicate rose is subtly integrated into the composition, accentuating her feminine grace and soft allure amidst her fierce presence. This artwork seamlessly blends the untamed force of nature with the timeless elegance of tradition, capturing the tigress as a sovereign whose roar commands respect, while her femininity radiates power and poise.

Rajwada Dwaar



"Rajwada Dwaar" is an exquisite depiction of a traditional Rajasthani gateway, showcasing intricate detailing and regal craftsmanship.







The ornate carvings and vibrant colors capture the essence of Rajasthan's royal heritage, while the majestic elephant standing at the entrance symbolizes strength, grandeur, and a warm welcome. This artwork embodies the spirit of royal tradition, evoking a sense of reverence and elegance, as if stepping into a world of timeless royalty and cultural opulence.

Anant



"Anant" is a stunning A3-sized painting that seamlessly blends the intricate beauty of Pichwai art with elements of divine symbolism. The composition features blooming lotuses, representing purity and spiritual enlightenment, while delicate bells evoke the sacred atmosphere of a temple, resonating with the call of devotion.

Vikas



This artwork blends the rich legacy of Indian culture with the dynamic flow of modern design, symbolizing the harmonious coexistence of tradition and progress. The vibrant colors and intricate patterns bring together timeless heritage and contemporary aesthetics, portraying how the past and future shape each other.

Shaan



The delicate miniature style brings forth the craftsmanship of a bygone era, while the colors of blue symbolize tranquility and royalty, perfectly complemented by the warmth of beige that adds a regal touch to the entire composition. This piece is a tribute to the grandeur and cultural richness of India's royal past, encapsulating the essence of the City Palace in every stroke.

Pravesh



A peaceful gateway leads to a serene lake, surrounded by lush greenery and blooming flowers, symbolizing harmony and tranquility.

Navkriti

My first painting exhibition, Navkriti where I displayed my artwork under the guidance of Mrs. Vasudha Sharma, a well-known painter and artist who gave me strength and a beautiful platform ( her studio) to showcase my talent.
